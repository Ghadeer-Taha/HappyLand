About Happy Land

Advanced manufacturing, authentic taste.

Happy Land Dairy Products is a leading Egyptian manufacturer specialising in the production and packaging of premium dairy products. Located in the 10th of Ramadan City, our factory combines advanced manufacturing technologies with internationally recognised food-safety and quality standards.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to delivering fresh, safe and high-quality dairy products that meet the expectations of both local and international markets. Our modern facilities, experienced technical team and strict quality-control systems ensure consistency and excellence in every product we manufacture.

Our portfolio includes Roumy Cheese, White Cheese, Cottage Cheese, Analogue Cheese, Natural Butter, Cow Ghee, Buffalo Ghee, Cream and a wide range of specialty dairy products. In addition to our own brands, we provide Private Label and OEM manufacturing solutions for regional and international partners.
