Quality control laboratory.

Quality assurance begins with raw-material inspection and continues through every stage of production until the final product reaches our customers.

Assured at Every Stage

Tested from raw milk to finished pack.

Our laboratory is equipped with modern analytical instruments and operated by qualified specialists to ensure that every product complies with national and international quality and food-safety standards.

All testing procedures are performed according to internationally recognised laboratory methods — ensuring product consistency, safety and customer confidence.

Our Laboratory Services

Four pillars of testing

Raw Milk Analysis

  • Acidity
  • Fat content
  • Protein
  • Density
  • SNF (Solids-Not-Fat)
  • Temperature & pH
  • Antibiotic residue detection

Finished Product Analysis

  • Moisture
  • Fat percentage
  • Protein
  • Salt content
  • Acidity
  • pH
  • Texture & firmness

Microbiological Testing

  • Total bacterial count
  • Coliform test
  • Yeast & mould detection
  • Pathogenic bacteria detection

Production Monitoring

  • Critical Control Point (CCP) monitoring
  • Temperature monitoring
  • Packaging inspection
  • Batch release verification
  • Production documentation
